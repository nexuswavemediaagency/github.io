What affects price

The quote follows the work

Two businesses can need the same service name but very different levels of work. That is why final pricing is scope-based.

01

Scope

How many pages, posts, campaigns, products, flows, or systems need to be built.

02

Platforms

Whether the work involves Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Google, website pages, WhatsApp, or ecommerce/catalog touchpoints.

03

Content needs

Whether assets already exist or the project needs writing, design, short-form editing, product copy, or campaign creative.

04

Timeline

Whether the work is a calm build, a launch sprint, ongoing support, or an international project with more coordination.
